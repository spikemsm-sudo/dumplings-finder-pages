Dumplings Finder Support

Help, privacy basics, and common questions for Dumplings Finder.

Dumplings Finder helps shoppers and collectors share recent shopper-reported sightings of squishy dumpling toys. Sightings are time-limited because store shelves can change quickly, and availability is never guaranteed.

Dumplings Finder is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Five Below or any product manufacturer.

Common Questions

Is Dumplings Finder official?

No. Store and product names are used only to describe shopper-reported sightings.

Are sightings guaranteed?

No. Sightings are shopper-reported. An item may be gone by the time someone arrives at a store.

Why did a sighting disappear?

Sightings expire automatically because they are meant to show recent reports. A sighting may also be marked gone if shoppers report that the item is no longer there.

Why does the app ask for location?

Location can help suggest nearby stores and alert areas. You can still use manual choices where available.

Why does the app ask for photo access?

Photo access is used only when you choose to add a photo to a sighting.

How do I report a bad or misleading sighting?

Use the report option in the app when available. You can also contact support with details about the sighting.
