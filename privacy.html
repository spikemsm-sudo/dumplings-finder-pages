Dumplings Finder Privacy Policy

Last updated: [month day, year]

Dumplings Finder helps users share and view recent shopper-reported sightings of squishy dumpling toys. Sightings may include store, product, price, shelf spot, notes, confirmations, reports, and an optional photo.

Dumplings Finder is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Five Below or any product manufacturer. Store and product names are used only to describe shopper-reported sightings, and availability is never guaranteed.

Information The App May Use

Location: With your permission, Dumplings Finder may use location to suggest nearby stores, show nearby sightings, and support alert radius features.

Photos: If you choose to add a photo to a sighting, the app may use that photo as part of the sighting report.

Photo details: When available and permitted, the app may use photo date or location information to suggest when and where a sighting happened. Suggested values should be shown before posting so you can edit them.

Sighting content: Sightings may include store location, product type, variant, size, price, shelf spot, notes, time posted, confirmations, reports, and an optional photo.

Notifications: If you allow notifications, the app may send alerts for matching nearby sightings.

Accounts or device identifiers: If accounts, device identity, push notifications, Firebase, or similar services are added, the app may use identifiers needed to operate those features.

How Information Is Used

  • Show recent shopper-reported sightings
  • Help users post sightings quickly
  • Suggest nearby stores when allowed
  • Send matching alerts when allowed
  • Help review reports or misleading posts
  • Improve app reliability and safety

What The App Does Not Do

Dumplings Finder should not sell personal information.

Dumplings Finder should not post hidden photo location information without showing the location being used.

Dumplings Finder does not guarantee that any item is available at a store.

User-Posted Content

Sightings are submitted by users. Do not post private information, inappropriate photos, or misleading reports. Public versions of the app should include a way to report content that appears inaccurate, inappropriate, or unsafe.

Children

Dumplings Finder is intended for general shopping and collector use. Do not knowingly collect personal information from children without any required consent.

Third-Party Services

Before launch, list any third-party services used by the final app, such as Firebase, push notification services, crash reporting, or analytics.

Current project signal: the app code currently imports Firebase Firestore and UserNotifications, so Firebase and push notifications should be treated as active unless removed from the submitted build.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact dumplingsfindersupport@gmail.com.
